**Term Glossary**
- `information architecture`: The structural design of how information is organized, labeled, and accessed
- `user-centered design`: Design approach that prioritizes user needs and behavior patterns
- `defensive UX`: Design strategy that anticipates and prevents common user errors
- `knowledge transfer`: Process of moving expertise and information between people or systems
- `decision-support tools`: Resources designed to help users make informed choices about actions

## A wrapper for Sunshine to make streaming to Moonlight devices simpler and easier. 

##### ***Use When***
- ##### Sunshine is being a pain in the butt
- ##### You don't want to deal with configuring virtual displays (configured automatically)
- ##### You don't want to have to go through the hassle of configuring app launching with sunshine (easier with Apollo )

#### Prerequisites
- Windows 11 PC
- Moonlight-capable client device to stream to

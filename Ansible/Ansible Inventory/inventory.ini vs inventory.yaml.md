### Choosing `.ini` vs choosing `.yaml` for your Ansible inventory

#### About Inventories
##### Ansible inventories are used to store host machine configurations. They tell Ansible which hosts to connect to, how to connect to them, and how to handle authentication. Inventories can store mulitiple host configurations which can be grouped logically by topology, location, and environment. You can use either `.ini` or `.yaml` to build your Ansible inventory, and while there are many similarites each comes with advantages and disadvantages.
